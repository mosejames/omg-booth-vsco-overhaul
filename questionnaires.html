Step 07
Review Gate
Not a form. An automation that reads Form 6 responses and routes the appropriate follow-up to the client. No staff action required after Form 6 is submitted.
Automation
Trigger
Form 6 (Post-Event Report) submission
Reads
Overall Event Rating + Client Satisfaction Notes
Output
One of two email paths (client-facing, AI-personalized)
Routing Logic
Rating 4-5 + positive satisfaction
Path A
Auto-trigger Google Review request email (AI-personalized with event details and highlights)
Rating 1-3 or flagged issues
Path B
Auto-trigger private feedback check-in email (no Google Review link included)
Why this matters: The review gate turns your internal debrief into a client touchpoint without adding another form or manual step. Staff fills out Form 6 like they already do. The system reads the data and sends the right email. Happy clients get a frictionless path to leave a Google Review. Rough events get a private, thoughtful check-in instead. No one on your team has to decide which email to send. The data decides.
