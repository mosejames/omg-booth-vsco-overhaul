Products, Services & Packages

Everything OMG Booth offers, organized by tier. Packages are built from actual order history across 929 deals and $2.36M in lifetime revenue. Every price listed here is backed by data, not guesswork.

$1,200
Minimum booking. All packages, custom quotes, and service configurations start at a $1,200 floor. This applies across every event type. If a lead's budget falls below this threshold, they should be redirected to a lighter option or declined.

Add-Ons

Smaller line items that round out a booking. These appear in the add-ons section of the upsell quote with quantity selectors. Low friction, high margin.

Additional Hour
Extend booth operating time beyond the package base. Most common add-on for corporate events and galas that run late.
$350/hr
Standby Hour
Booth is on-site but inactive. Covers setup buffer, dinner service, speeches, or ceremony breaks where the booth pauses.
$150/hr
Step-and-Repeat Backdrop
Custom branded backdrop with logos and event design. Available as a standalone add-on for Essential and Premium packages.
$500
Lead Capture Module
Data collection at the booth: name, email, phone, and custom fields. Exportable lead list delivered post-event. Standard in Full Activation.
$300
Same-Day Content Package
Edited highlight reel of booth content delivered the same day as the event. Perfect for social media teams that need content fast.
$500
Post-Event Analytics Report
Engagement metrics, content performance stats, and lead data export. Branded PDF delivered within 48 hours. Standard in Full Activation.
