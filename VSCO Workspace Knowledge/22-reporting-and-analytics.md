# VSCO Workspace - Reporting and Analytics

## Overview

Reports are automatically generated as you use your account. Standard (non-admin) users cannot see the Reports menu but can see individual financial data on each job and pricing on products/quotes.

## Report Types

### Booking & Revenue Analytics
- Analyze booking trends by job type, date, and client
- Track product and service sales over time
- See which packages generate the most revenue
- **Product Booking Report** -- Track product sales history

### Lead & Sales Tracking
- Track leads, conversion rates, and sales
- **Booking Trends by Lead Source** -- Analyze which marketing strategies work
- Lead source tracking tied to custom job roles
- Job conversion rates (affected by proper use of Completed vs. Closed)

### Financial Reporting
- Revenue, expenses, profit, overhead tracking
- Outstanding invoices
- Profit and Loss by Brand
- **Profit Centers** dashboard report

---

## Profit Centers

**Location:** Dashboard widget + Reports > Accounting > Profit Centers

### What They Show:
- **Year-to-date stats**: Total revenue, profits, overhead, number of jobs
- Breakdown of jobs booked for surrounding years per profit center

### What They Are:
- Categories of work at your studio (can be broad or specific)
- Assigned to Job Types
- Help segment your business for analysis

### Access:
- Dashboard widget shows overview
- Full report via "Full Report" button or Reports > Accounting > Profit Centers

---

## Referral Tracking

### Setup:
1. Create a "Referral" job role in Settings > Job Roles
2. Tie a person to a job as a referral using the custom job role
3. Track all booked jobs from their contact card's Leads & Jobs tab

### Automation:
- Integrate referral tracking into workflows
- Automate rewards (credit, thank-you emails)
- Track referral sources over time

---

## Lead Source Tracking

**Location:** Settings > Lead Sources

- Pre-populated with common sources
- Detailed analysis in Booking Trends by Lead Source report
- Helps identify which marketing channels are most effective

---

## Reporting Best Practices

- Always use **Completed** (not Closed) for successfully fulfilled jobs to keep reports accurate
- Closing jobs improperly causes inaccuracies in conversion rates and financial reports
- Reports by Brand available for multi-brand accounts
- Use custom tags and filters to create targeted report views
