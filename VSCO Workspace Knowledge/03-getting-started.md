# VSCO Workspace - Getting Started

## Quick Setup Checklist

### Step 1: Brand Settings
- Go to Settings > Branding
- Add a logo and email signature
- Configure the "Look & Feel" of your brand
- Preview your brand settings

### Step 2: Taxes and Currency
- Go to Settings > Taxes & Currency
- Add any sales tax rates your clients may be subject to
- Set your default currency

### Step 3: Client Payment Methods
- Go to Settings > Client Payment Methods
- Add "Mail a Check" as minimum offline option
- For online payments: Connect Stripe or Square

### Step 4: Create Your First Lead
- Go to the dashboard > New Lead
- Enter job date (if established)
- Enter name and email for at least one contact
- Enter any other collected information
- Leads can also come in automatically through contact forms

### Step 5: Build Your First Quote
- Go to the lead from the leads sidebar
- Click Quotes & Orders > New Quote
- Add recipient, quote name, message, expiration, sales tax group
- Add products/services, payment schedule, questionnaire, and contract

### Step 6: Customize a Contact Form
- Go to Settings > Contact Forms
- Customize the form to embed on your website

### Step 7: Set Up Workflows
- Go to Settings > Workflow
- Build workflows with task lists for each job stage
- Or install pre-built workflows from industry professionals with one click

### Step 8: Configure Job Types
- Go to Settings > Job Types
- Assign workflows, job roles, and event types to each job type
