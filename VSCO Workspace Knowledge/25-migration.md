# VSCO Workspace - CRM Migration

## Overview

VSCO Workspace offers migration support for users switching from other CRMs.

**URL:** https://www.vsco.co/workspace/crm-migration

## Process

1. **Free Discovery Call** -- Understand your existing workflow and goals
2. **Tailored Migration Plan** -- Personalized to your needs
3. **One or two personalized, hour-long sessions** to help make the most of your new account

## Migration Tiers

1. **Lite** -- Basic migration
2. **Standard** -- More comprehensive
3. **Custom** -- Full custom migration

Every plan starts with a free discovery call where the onboarding team assesses your setup and provides a transparent, personalized quote.

## What Can Be Migrated

- Projects
- Client information
- Contracts
- Questionnaires
- Templates
- Branding
- And more

**Note:** Some elements may need to be rebuilt or adapted to fit Workspace's system.

## Supported Migrations From

- HoneyBook
- Dubsado
- Studio Ninja
- Other CRM platforms
- Spreadsheets

## Tave to VSCO Workspace Transition

For existing Tave users:
- No migration required -- all data preserved automatically
- Users simply create or connect a VSCO account as login method
- All automations, emails, workflows, and integrations remain intact
- No subscription increases through 2025-2026
- Complimentary one-year VSCO Pro membership included
