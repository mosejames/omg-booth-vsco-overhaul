# VSCO Workspace - Overview and History

## What Is VSCO Workspace?

VSCO Workspace is the most customizable CRM for photographers -- an organizational platform designed to be the hub of your photography business. It covers the full client lifecycle from inquiry to delivery.

Core capabilities:
- Lead tracking and CRM
- Online scheduling and booking
- Automated workflows
- Client communication (email and two-way SMS texting)
- Invoicing and online payments
- Contracts and questionnaires
- Performance reporting and analytics
- Client portal
- Multiple brand management
- Gallery integrations (ShootProof, Pic-Time)

## History: Tave to VSCO Workspace

- **May 2025**: VSCO acquired Tave's Studio Manager and all 6 team members
- **August 19, 2025**: VSCO Workspace officially launched (World Photography Day)
- All 6 Tave team members joined VSCO in their same roles; Workspace team size doubled
- All existing data, automations, emails, workflows, and integrations preserved -- no migration required
- Users simply created or connected a VSCO account as new login method
- No subscription increases for legacy Tave customers through 2025-2026
- All paying Tave customers received a one-year VSCO Pro membership
- The API continues to work without redirect

## Target Users

- Wedding and portrait photographers
- Videographers
- Event professionals
- Solo operators to multi-person studios
- Scales from solopreneurs to large studios with multiple brands

## How It Compares to Competitors

- **vs. HoneyBook**: Workspace Solo ($25/mo) cheaper than HoneyBook Starter ($36/mo). Deeper automation and customization; HoneyBook is simpler for solo photographers.
- **vs. Dubsado**: Both support custom CSS. Workspace offers more advanced workflow automation and reporting.
- **vs. Studio Ninja**: Workspace provides more features and customization; Studio Ninja focuses on simplicity.
- **Best for**: Larger studios with 5+ team members, multi-brand operations, photographers wanting deep workflow automation and granular control.

## VSCO Pro vs VSCO Workspace

VSCO Pro and VSCO Workspace are two separate subscriptions:
- **VSCO Pro**: Photo/video editing app membership ($59.99/year)
- **VSCO Workspace**: Studio management CRM (separate pricing tiers)
