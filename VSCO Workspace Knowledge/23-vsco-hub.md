# VSCO Workspace - VSCO Hub

## What It Is

VSCO Hub is a marketplace that connects brands and businesses with professional photographers from the VSCO community. Described as "LinkedIn for Photographers." Launched in 2024.

## AI-Powered Search

Features a human-centered, AI-powered search system that finds photographers matching a brand's desired style and aesthetic.

### Search Methods:
- Upload a **reference image** to find matching styles
- Search by **location** and **experience**
- (Coming soon) **Text queries** for semantic image search

## How It Works for Photographers

1. Join **VSCO Pro Membership** ($59.99/year, 160,000+ subscribers)
2. Update the **availability tag** on your profile to "Professional Work"
3. Go to the "Available for" section of your profile
4. Post examples of work showcasing your style
5. Include a **general price range** so brands can gauge budget fit
6. The more you post, the more likely you appear in relevant searches

## How It Works for Brands/Businesses

1. Must use an **official business email**
2. Businesses are **vetted by VSCO** before joining
3. Create account; once confirmed, start searching immediately
4. Browse photographers and click "Request To Connect"
5. VSCO's Hub team contacts the photographer from the vsco-for-work account
6. If available, VSCO connects both parties via email
7. Work opportunity discussed freely from there

## Pricing Model

- VSCO Members set their own pricing for their work
- VSCO Hub facilitates the connection only
- **VSCO does not take a cut** from project payments -- purely a connection layer

## Safety

- Always verify who is connecting with you
- Report suspicious messages/accounts to VSCO's Trust & Safety Team
- Be wary of: promises of easy money, upfront payment requests, signup fees
- Ask for company email verification; check domain legitimacy
- Block and report suspected scammers

## Future Integration

VSCO plans to embed Workspace directly into the VSCO experience to seamlessly transition from showcasing work on VSCO to managing client inquiries through Workspace.
