# VSCO Workspace - Client Portal

## Overview

The Client Portal is a single link where clients can access all their information in one place.

## What Clients See:
- Open leads and jobs
- Financial items (invoices, payment status)
- Upcoming interactions/events
- Quotes, contracts, questionnaires
- Payment pages

## Features:
- Preview a contact's Client Portal from their Contact Profile Overview
- All client documents consolidated in one page
- Fully branded with your business's look and feel

## Portal Token:
- Use `{{recipient.portal_linked}}` in emails to include a link to the client portal
- Use `{{brand.client_portal_linked}}` for the brand's portal link
- Use `{{brand.client_portal_url}}` for the raw URL

## Client Access Pages:
Client Access refers to any page where clients interact with Workspace:
- Contact forms
- Questionnaires
- Contracts
- Quotes
- Invoices
- Payment pages
- Client portal

All are branded consistently with your brand settings.

## Customization:
- Configure settings for client access in the branding section
- Option to show questionnaires in the client portal after initial submission
- Customize footer with contact details, terms of service, privacy policy
- Each brand has its own look and feel for client-facing pages
