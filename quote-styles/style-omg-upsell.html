To get the card layout with product images in VSCO Workspace:
  • Add images to your products — Go to Settings > Products & Services, edit each product, upload a photo. This is what makes the card layout appear.
  • Use Upgradable Quotes — When building a quote, create an "Advanced Quote" with tiered packages. Clients see options and can select/add.
  • Set up add-ons as separate products — Create individual products for each enhancement (Additional Hour, Step-and-Repeat, etc.) and add them to the quote as optional items.
  • Add product badges — Product descriptions support text that appears below the image. Use compelling copy like the screenshot example.
  • Payment schedule — Set up a 50/50 payment schedule (retainer + balance) in the quote. Workspace auto-generates two invoices.
  • Include questionnaire + contract — Add your Event Details questionnaire and contract to the quote flow. Client sees: pricing > questionnaire > contract > payment in one seamless link.
